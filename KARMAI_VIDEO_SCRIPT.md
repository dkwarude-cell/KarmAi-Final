# KarmAI: The Operating System for Campus Life

## Detailed Video Pitch & Technical Master Script

**Target Audience:** Venture Capitalists, Angel Investors, Technical Judges (Hackathons/Grants), and University Administrators.
**Visual Style:** High-end, cinematic, fluid transitions. Tech elements use glowing "Glassmorphism" UI panels, sleek 3D architectural renders of college campuses, and dynamic data flow visualizations (pulsing vectors, neural networks).
**Pacing:** Starts grounded and empathetic, accelerates into deep technical sophistication, and culminates in explosive, undeniable business metrics.
