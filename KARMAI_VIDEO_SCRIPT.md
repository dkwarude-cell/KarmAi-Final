# KarmAI: The Operating System for Campus Life

## 2-Minute Video Pitch & Technical Master Script

**Target Audience:** Venture Capitalists, Angel Investors, Technical Judges (Hackathons/Grants).
**Visual Style:** High-end, cinematic, fluid transitions. Tech elements use glowing "Glassmorphism" UI panels, sleek 3D architectural renders of college campuses.
**Total Runtime:** 2 Minutes (120 Seconds)

---

## 🎬 SCENE 1: The Hook & Introduction (0:00 - 0:20)

**⏱️ Time:** 20 Seconds

**🎥 VISUALS:**

- Sweeping drone shot of an Indian campus. Kinetic typography: `"India: 43 Million Students."`
- Cut to Priya (20, student). She is looking at her phone, overwhelmed by a dozen floating app icons (food delivery, social media, maps, portals).
- The clutter shatters. Replaced by a single, sleek glowing icon: **KarmAI**.

**🎤 AUDIO (VO):**
_"India has 43 million college students juggling a dozen different apps just to manage their day. Meet Priya. Today, we’re walking through a day in her life using KarmAI—the unified, AI-driven operating system that bridges her physical and digital campus."_

---

## 🎬 SCENE 2: The Map & Contextual AI Demo (0:20 - 0:50)

**⏱️ Time:** 30 Seconds

**🎥 VISUALS:**

- Priya opens the KarmAI Map. It pulses: `"GPS Active"`.
- She taps a glowing map pin for 'Aahar Canteen'. Instantly, a bottom-sheet pops up with contextual buttons: **"Ask AI"**, and **"Order Now"**.
- **[API EXAMPLE VISUALIZED]** She taps "Ask AI". The AI Concierge replies: _"Perfect for breakfast under ₹80. It's 2 mins away before your 8 AM lecture."_
- She taps **"Order Now"**. A golden coin drops into the KarmAI Wallet.

**🎤 AUDIO (VO):**
_"At 8 AM, Priya needs a fast breakfast. Our Live Map doesn't just show locations—it understands context. By clicking the canteen, the system dynamically generates 'Order Now' and 'Ask AI' modules. The AI confirms the meal fits her budget and schedule block. When she orders directly, KarmAI takes a transparent B2B cut. High-intent matchmaking, zero forced ads."_

## 🎬 SCENE 3: Discovery Feed & Partner Dashboards (0:50 - 1:20)

**⏱️ Time:** 30 Seconds

**🎥 VISUALS:**

- Cut to Priya scrolling the **Discovery Feed** (Instagram Reels style). She saves a post tagging a local cafe.
- Split screen: We see the **Restaurant Partner Dashboard**. The cafe manager launches a campaign: _"Weekend Coffee Drift"_.
- Priya walks near the cafe later. Her phone buzzes: _"Serendipitous Detour: 20% off your saved cafe."_

**🎤 AUDIO (VO):**
_"By midday, Priya browses our peer-driven Discovery Feed. Meanwhile, what fuels this economy? Our Partner Dashboards. Local businesses can inject 'Drift Campaigns' directly into the map. When students like Priya walk nearby, the app triggers a serendipitous detour exactly when they intend to buy. It’s an absolute paradigm shift for campus marketing."_
