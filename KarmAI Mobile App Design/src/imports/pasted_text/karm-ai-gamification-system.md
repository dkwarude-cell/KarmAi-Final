Design an advanced gamification and reward system for KarmAI that creates addictive progression loops while maintaining the core philosophy of "real-world action, not screen time."

REQUIREMENTS:

1. MULTI-TIER KARMA SYSTEM
- Base Karma: Earned from GPS-verified visits (50-200 points)
- Bonus Multipliers:
  * First Visit Bonus: 2x karma
  * Distance Bonus: +10% per km beyond 5km
  * Comfort Zone Bonus: +50% if outside preferred categories
  * Time Challenge: +25% for early morning (6-9am) or late night (9pm-12am) visits
  * Group Visit: +30% when visiting with connections
  * Streak Multiplier: 1.1x per consecutive day (max 2x at 10 days)
  * Rarity Bonus: Rare locations give 3x karma
  
2. DYNAMIC BADGE SYSTEM
Design 50+ unique badges across categories:
- **Distance Badges**: Local Hero (1km), City Explorer (10km), Road Tripper (50km+)
- **Category Master**: Complete all locations in one category
- **Social Badges**: Connector (5 people), Network Builder (20 people), Community Leader (50 people)
- **Time-based**: Night Owl, Early Bird, Weekend Warrior
- **Combo Badges**: Visit 3 different categories in one day
- **Seasonal**: Monsoon Explorer, Summer Adventurer
- **Rare**: Lucky 7 (visit on 7th day of month at 7pm), Serendipity Master (100% AI match visit)

Include badge progression: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond levels

3. ADVANCED RANKING SYSTEM
Multiple leaderboards:
- Global Karma Leaderboard (all-time)
- Monthly Challenge Board (resets monthly)
- College vs College Rankings
- Category-Specific Rankings (Heritage Champion, Foodie King, etc.)
- Friend Group Rankings
- Weekly Active Leaderboard
- "Rising Star" board (biggest gains this week)

Ranking Tiers:
- Novice (0-500 karma)
- Explorer (500-1500)
- Pathfinder (1500-3000)
- Adventurer (3000-6000)
- Trailblazer (6000-10000)
- Legend (10000+)

Each tier unlocks:
- Exclusive badge designs
- Special profile customization
- Early access to new locations
- Higher karma multipliers
- VIP event invitations
- Direct partnership deals

4. REWARD MARKETPLACE
Implement a sophisticated rewards economy:

**Instant Rewards** (Low Karma):
- Coffee/tea vouchers (200-500 karma)
- Food delivery discounts (300-600 karma)
- Transportation credits (400 karma)

**Experience Rewards** (Medium Karma):
- Museum/gallery passes (800-1200 karma)
- Movie tickets (600 karma)
- Workshop registrations (1000 karma)
- Escape room entries (1500 karma)

**Premium Rewards** (High Karma):
- Weekend getaway packages (5000 karma)
- Concert VIP passes (3000 karma)
- Exclusive dining experiences (2500 karma)
- Adventure activity bundles (4000 karma)

**Limited Edition** (Very High Karma):
- Meet local celebrities/influencers (10000 karma)
- Private tours of restricted areas (8000 karma)
- Lifetime partner memberships (15000 karma)

**Time-Limited Offers**:
- Flash deals (50% karma discount, 24hr only)
- Seasonal specials
- Partner promotions

5. PROGRESSION MECHANICS
Level System (1-100):
- XP curve: exponential growth (level 1 = 100 XP, level 10 = 5000 XP, level 50 = 100000 XP)
- Each level unlock:
  * New profile customization options
  * Increased karma earning rate (+2% per level)
  * Access to exclusive locations
  * New badge slots (showcase limit)
  * Friend list expansion

Prestige System:
- At level 50, option to "prestige" and restart with:
  * Special prestige badge
  * Permanent 50% karma multiplier
  * Exclusive cosmetic rewards
  * Legacy status on leaderboard

6. SOCIAL COMPETITIVE FEATURES
- Direct challenges: Challenge friends to visit specific locations
- Team quests: Group missions with shared rewards
- Rivalry system: Compete 1v1 for weekly karma crown
- College pride: Collective karma contributes to college ranking
- Mentor program: High-level users can mentor newcomers for bonus karma

7. ACHIEVEMENT TRACKING
Progress dashboards showing:
- Total distance traveled (with city comparisons: "You've walked from Mumbai to Pune!")
- Categories completion percentage
- Rarest locations visited
- Longest streak
- Most karma earned in single day
- Social connection graph
- Comfort zone expansion visualization

8. REWARD REDEMPTION UX
- QR code generation for instant redemption
- Partner app integration
- Expiration date management
- Gift rewards to friends
- Karma donation to charity (partner with NGOs)
- Trade rewards with other users (karma marketplace)

9. ANTI-GAMING MEASURES
To prevent abuse while maintaining engagement:
- GPS spoofing detection (cross-reference movement patterns)
- Time-based cooldowns (max 5 check-ins per day)
- Diminishing returns (same location gives 50% karma on repeat visit within 7 days)
- Human verification (random photo proof requests: "Take a photo with this landmark")
- Behavior pattern analysis (flag suspicious rapid check-ins)

10. NOTIFICATIONS & TRIGGERS
Smart push notifications:
- "You're 50 karma away from [Reward Name]!"
- "[Friend Name] just overtook you on the leaderboard!"
- "Double karma weekend starts in 2 hours!"
- "New location unlocked near you!"
- "Your streak is about to break! Visit today!"
- "You're ranked #12, climb to top 10 for exclusive rewards!"

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY:
- Every reward must reinforce real-world action
- No dark patterns or addictive loops that keep users on screens
- Transparency in all progression systems
- Social motivation without FOMO toxicity
- Celebrate exploration, not exploitation

4. Reward pricing strategy

6. Anti-cheat mechanisms
